All my papers can be found on [DBLP](https://dblp.org/pid/299/8207.html) or [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=2G4gViMAAAAJ).
<br/>

I work very closely with [Jonathan Shi](https://jshi.science/) on obstructing algorithms in the presence of ultrametric OGPs and designing unifying sum-of-squares (SoS) relaxations for spin glasses and random Max-CSPs. Some questions and techniques that excite me include the <a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/1309.7495.pdf">qPCP conjecture</a>, <a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/2110.07847.pdf">ultrametric OGPs</a>, <a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/2210.03006.pdf">Guerra-Tonnineli interpolations</a>, <a href="https://www.dsteurer.org/paper/expansion.pdf">small-set expansion/unique-games conjectures</a> and <a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/1512.02337.pdf">analytic sparsity proofs</a>.
